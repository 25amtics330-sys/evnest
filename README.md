## 🌍 The Problem

As electric vehicles (EVs) become increasingly popular, the infrastructure to support them struggles to keep pace. 
EV owners often face **"range anxiety"**—the fear of running out of battery before finding a charging station. Public charging stations are frequently:
- ❌ Overcrowded with long wait times
- ❌ Out of service or broken
- ❌ Inconveniently located far from destinations

Simultaneously, thousands of private home chargers sit idle for hours every day, representing a massive, untapped resource.

## 💡 The Solution: EVNest

**EVNest** is an innovative peer-to-peer marketplace that bridges the gap between EV owners and charger hosts. Think of it as *"Airbnb for EV Charging"*.

By democratizing the charging infrastructure, we:
- ✅ **Empower EV Owners** to find reliable, pre-bookable charging spots anywhere.
- ✅ **Enable Hosts** to monetize their idle home charging equipment.
- ✅ **Accelerate the Green Transition** by reducing range anxiety and making EV ownership accessible to everyone.

## 🛠️ Technology Architecture

Our stack is built for speed, scalability, and premium developer/user experience.

- **Frontend Design:** React 19, Vite, Tailwind CSS, React Leaflet
- **Backend APIs:** Node.js, Express.js, JWT Authentication
- **Database:** MongoDB / Mongoose Data Modeling
- **Deployment:** Vercel (Edge Client Delivery), Render (Backend Node Runtime)
